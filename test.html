How To Play
The objective of the game is to score three small boards in a row (so, like a normal game of Tic-Tac-Toe). Each of those small boards must be won themselves.
The trick comes down to placement. You can only place an X or O in the board that corresponds to your opponent's last square.
For example, if I played in the top right square of my board, you'd only be able to play in the top right board.
If you're forced into a board that's already been won, or one that's full, you can play wherever you want.
